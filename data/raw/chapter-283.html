Heretic Questioner.

Yes!

Yes! I am!

Hmm.

If youre talking about physical pain and psychological trauma, yes, I think I was quite troubled in the past.

Yes!

Kim Gong-ja of the Demonic Sect.

Its not just the strong you need to defeat in this world, nor just the weak you need to help.

Venomous Snake addressed me not simply as the Death King or Kim Gong-ja, but specifically as [Kim Gong-ja of the Demonic Sect], acknowledging that he was facing not just a hunter from the Tower, not just my martial prowess, but the weight of the Demonic Sect and the expanse of the Infernal Heavens I bore.

Your Demonic Sect took the cries of the common people as its doctrine. Those cries became revenge, and the revenge turned into bloodshed, staining the world red. Bloodthirsty avengers! The world is already soaked in blood, yet you still pretend to be the weak.

This was a declaration of war from the 990th duel.

It was similar to what the Martial Arts Leader Namgung Un had said to my master, the leader of the Demonic Sect.

Did you always cling to the Heavenly Demons coat, crying endlessly? Begging for someone else to perform the rites of death on your behalf. While clinging to the sleeves, you considered yourselves weak commoners, sad people.

Right. Youre not the Heavenly Demon, and people around dont call you that, but I want to call you that.

Damn it! My foot, it hurts!

Another clang of metal was heard.

Not an unusual sound and something that could easily be mimicked with aura, but I intuitively felt there was some connection between Venomous Snakes joyful laughter and the metallic clangs.

The reason he elevated our duel to a battle between the Demonic Sect and the Orthodox Faction.
