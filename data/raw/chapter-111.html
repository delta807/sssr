I couldn't understand.

I couldn't understand what the Lady of the Silver Lily was saying, and I couldn't understand my own feelings. Why did my heart feel sick? Did she drug me? I was familiar with various poisons due to attending to the Lady of the Golden Silk.

But this symptom… I'd never heard of it.
