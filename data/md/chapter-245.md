I returned Estelle's body, returned the bodies of the members of the Demonic Cult, allowed Sylvia Evanail to have a life, and finally, embraced the life of Kim Yul. I, as I carried their greed, desire, wishes and hope— became clearer.

Became heavy.

Broader.

[May luck be with you.]

God, who wanted to be the mother of everyone born into the world.

I.

Thought that was sad.

"If it was up to me, you wouldn't have to!"

Adjust the hilt of my sword, I looked up at the sky.

In the past, I was worried if the Tower could hear the voice of a guy like me. However, I wasn't now. Every word I said, every word I would say, would reach the ears of Tower— of Ja Soo-jung.

Because I was now that kind of existence.

I sat on the living room floor.

Only Raviel and I were sitting on the floor, so it was clear who had the right to sit on the throne.

"The name of our family clan will be the Death King Family(死王家)."

"I am also the Family Head of a Great Family Clan."

"You are my eldest daughter, the successor who will lead the family in my stead in the event of an emergency."

"..."

"Emergency... is a disrespectful word. The Family Head will never die..."

"I, of course, have a secret. Losing my life one or two times won't be enough to cause a crisis for our family clan. However, that doesn't mean that we shouldn't prepare for emergency situations. "Yes."

She grabbed her blade with both hands, then pressed her forehead to the crossguard.

"I, Estelle, will be the Successor of the Death King Family."

"Good."

We might still be inexperienced as parent and child.

However, if it was a relationship as [Family Head] and [Successor], we could do that well.

This was a start.

Normal people became parent and child first then head of the household and successor. But that was only for those who had normal luck.

After being broken into pieces, we had been scattered across the world, and only met each other by accident, in reverse. We had no choice but to become a family backwards.

That was enough.

The difficulty beyond the 50th floor would become incredibly severe.

Because I was the only one who had come face to face with Ja Soo-jung.

Only I knew what kind of person the Owner of the Tower was, what she thought, and what she wanted.

"This dad is good at rescuing and uniting people. I can boast that I am quite competent in that regard. Dad can tell what kind of scars a person has by looking at them, and what I can't tell from looking at them, I can tell when I die."
