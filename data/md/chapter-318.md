6.

A lot of things happened over the course of a fortnight.

[The constellation The Whip That Descends on the Self-Abuser has regained its stars.]

[The constellation Fist Holding the Sun is recruiting new followers for the first time in 233 years!]

[The constellation Guardian of the Southeast Gate has been released from its seal.]

[The constellation Failed Monologue has been released from its seal.]

Firstly, the five constellations sealed by the Magic Tower were liberated.

Not all constellations were fortunate enough to have admirable followers like the [Berserker].

For periods ranging from 900 to 200 years, these constellations had been sealed. Some constellations, once glorious, had declined to the point of having not even a single follower left.

The last follower who served me died 302 years ago, muttered one constellation.

It was a beautiful constellation with black hair. The hair was so long that it flowed across the entire rooms carpet. The [Claws Scratching the Night Sky], true to its alias, looked up into the void with eyes that resembled the night sky.

Originally, I was a minor constellation.

What kind of authority do you have?

Theres not much to call an authority. If, in your life, youve ever laid in bed, or not in bed, and stretched out your hand towards the night sky, attempting to scratch it with your fingernails, that act would be considered faith towards me.

.

How about it?

The constellation of the night sky smiled silently.

Not so minor, is it?

No, its amazing. Not like pretending to be a god or anything. Why would the Grey Spider seal such a constellation?

Who knows. She must have had her own reasons. Maybe she was annoyed every time a message about me appeared when stretching towards the night sky. Or perhaps she coveted my power because Im a constellation. Maybe it was to keep me alive.

Really?

Either way, I dont hate her.

The constellation, who until a few days ago was nailed to a cross and drained of magic for hundreds of years, gazed out the window with a shadowed look.

Hatred is a potent energy. Both the sender and the receiver become more distinct. Just like love, greed, or hope, it keeps someone who should die alive. The Death King. Our savior. Do you understand what Im saying?

Yes.

I nodded, recalling the eternity of 4000 days.

I understand.

I locked eyes with the night skys gaze.

I know well.

Many things happened.

The spiders lost their spire but gained a mountain-like arena, and additionally, the right to migrate to the Lion World. The Snail Race demonstrated their architectural prowess, creating living quarters for the spiders.

The destinations for migration varied. Some wanted to live in the New Continent, some in Raviels Empire, others in Babylon, and so on.

Most of them are magicians who have lived for over a hundred years! They are high-quality talents!

The Black Dragon Witch, who had always suffered from a perpetual shortage of manpower, rejoiced.

Can we recruit these people as we please? Huh?

Theres no particular issue. Just dont stop anyone who challenges them to a duel.

Huh? What does that mean?

I explained.

After hearing the whole story, the Black Dragon Witch lamented.

Why cant we have people who are both morally and skillfully competent in any world? I know I shouldnt be the one to say this.

Im here, arent I?

Yeah. You shouldnt be the one to say that.

The Black Dragon Witch lay down and sighed deeply.

Alright. Ive said it before, but Im not in a position to judge others Anyway, youve worked hard.

Is everything fine here?

Focus on your own problems. Well handle issues here. Thats right, isnt it? Hamustra said he wanted to gather our achievements and create a spin-off.

No, whats that.

Ive entered the mukbang genre. How about Black Dragon Food Fighter for the title? Its a story about me traveling through various worlds and just eating delicious food. Itll sell, right?

Seriously, what I can see why Anastasia needs some healing. Please take it easy and rest. Im leaving now.

So, whether Black Dragon Food Fighter will be created or not remains to be seen, but the competition to recruit the witch race definitely heated up.

Even considering the unavoidable life-or-death duel requests, the magicians were undoubtedly valuable talents.

Ill offer you double the salary of any offer youve received so far.

The final winner of this competition was the Count.

Moreover, in our alliance, there are talented individuals from the entire Elf race. Its like having a host of capable subordinates ready to be commanded. Black Dragon, Ten Thousand Temple, all good places with their own merits. But why should one go there to work themselves to death under the pretense of responsibility?

I swear my loyalty! Wah! King!

Right, right. You understand what Im saying. But could you cry like a cat for me?

Meow! Meow! And, could you please protect my life too? I really dont want to do any duels

Thats impossible.

Damn it.

Thus, some of the spiders who had originally sworn allegiance to the Magic Tower, then to me, switched their loyalty to the alliance. All within a mere fortnight. Isnt that a prime example of the unpredictability of life?

However, there were spiders who didnt align themselves with anyone.

How could I do that.

Naturally, it was the former leader of the old Magic Tower, the Grey Spider.

Do you have any plans?

I have a responsibility to bear.

The Grey Spider spoke while drinking plain water from a paper cup.

Have you seen the duel requests that came for me?

Yes.

I nodded, recalling the considerable amount of paper that made me seriously worry about the scarcity of trees on the 50th floor.

There were a lot.

Yes. And they will keep coming I have to accept them. To tie up loose ends. Thats the only way to move forward. Do you understand what Im saying?

The Grey Spiders words had a double meaning.

Among those who sent duel requests, there would definitely be people who couldnt sleep unless they ripped apart the Grey Spiders silk or saw her dead body. People who would feel hot water as cold until they witnessed her demise. People who couldnt move forward a single step without settling their score with the Grey Spider in some way.

Yes.

Again, I nodded, recalling the eternity of 4000 days.

I understand what you mean.

And so, the Grey Spider chose the vast, empty wasteland as her new nest. Anyone who bore a grudge against her could come there to request a duel.

She would hardly have days alone.

But if ever that day comes.

Even if a thousand years pass a thousand times, it may still seem far off, but if really, someday, there comes a day when no one seeks her out from morning till night. If she is granted the luxury of sipping a cup of water for an hour in the stretched sunlight of midday.

Only then would the Grey Spider be able to move on. To rise from the place she had stayed for a thousand years and finally head towards the next world.

I asked her.

What would you like to do when that day comes?

The Grey Spider smiled.

In that smile, I recalled someone. Someone who knew they could die at any moment and therefore didnt put on airs or feel regret anymore. There was a pure brightness born from complete acceptance in that smile.

Who knows. If Im that lucky.

The Grey Spider looked upwards.

It was nighttime, and the place she had chosen to stay had no ceiling, so the starry night spread infinitely above.

The Grey Spider reached out with her dry fingers towards it.

If Im that lucky, I could climb the tower.

The tower.

Youre going to try again?

Ive decided not to wait any longer.

I didnt ask any more questions.

The Grey Spider didnt say anything more either.

The three of us just looked up at the same night sky.
