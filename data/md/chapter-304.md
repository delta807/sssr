-Hmm.

It was evident in his eyes. The eyes of the Sword Emperor were clear. They were the eyes that would boldly accept the toxicity of any wound he received.

-Interesting.

He did not think of himself as a [body too precious to be wounded]. Nor did he boast of being a [body that need not suffer wounds]. He simply always strived to become stronger. A monster-like pride lurked in this mans heart.

-But, I shall refuse.

Looking around at the starlight flocking to him, the Sword Emperor smirked.

-I will not choose anyone! I wont pick any constellation! I will climb to the 100th floor, see the view there before anyone else, keep it to myself, and then remain silent. I wont tell any of you anything!

With his hair red as the sun, the Sword Emperor laughed.

-If youre curious about whats up there, stand up and follow me! Friendship is not about climbing a mountain together. Only those who have climbed the same mountain are entitled to share friendship!

The Sword Emperor vanished into the distance, becoming a starlight himself.

99th floor.

The place where he fell like a meteor was just one step away from reaching the top.

People could not find his remains. It was impossible to ascend to the 99th floor. Even the Sword Emperors followers, who arrogantly called themselves his devotees, couldnt get close.

Was he really dead? Or was he still on the 99th floor? Perhaps, he reached the 100th floor and disappeared without a trace.

The tower revealed nothing. Only rumors were rampant.
