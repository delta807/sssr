"Why did you only gather people like this and make them your lovers?"

Tower Master Ja Soo-jung.

She would one day build a Tower that gathered all of the abandoned people from all over the world. Children who were made victims by religious fanatics, warriors forced to die by unreasonable disasters, women whose lives were ruined by mysterious circumstances… All of those whose lives were abandoned or ruined, were gathered and brought to a new world.
