1.

One winter day.

So Baek-hyang followed her mother across a snowy field.

It was cloudy. Snow poured out of the cloudy sky. In the pouring snowstorm, people were but shadows.

Her mother's back, too, was a shadow-like haze.

So Baek-hyang raised her voice.

-Mother!

So Baek-hyang suddenly became afraid. The sound of snow was too loud. Her voice was covered by the sound of the falling snow, so she could not cry very loudly.

-Mother!

Her mother's shadow was still far away.

So Baek-hyang became impatient.

She had to walk faster to chase after her mother. However, the further she walked, the more breathless she became. The more she walked, the smaller her voice. What should she do?

-Uh…

Should she stop yelling and walk faster to catch up with her mother? Or should she stop walking and scream as loud as she can?

The young Heavenly Demon did not know.

While she was debating what to do, the shadow withdrew a little further away.

-Mother!

Eventually, So Baek-hyang stopped.

-Mother!

As she stopped, her voice became a little stronger. Until now, her energy had been focused on walking not on directing her voice. So Baek-hyang thought that finally her mother would turn her head and look at her, and the shadow would become clear.

But what happened?

The shadow got a little further away.

While So Baek-hyang screamed, it had gained more distance.

Before long, the shadow of her mother was buried in the blizzard and disappeared into the snowfield.

-······.

Did her mother abandon her?

Had she intended to part ways with her while using the snowstorm as a pretext?

Or did her mother really lose her by accident?

So Baek-hyang did not know.

She couldn't know.
